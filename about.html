Who We Are

A Legacy of
Watchmaking

Rooted in Guangzhou's renowned watch trade district, we bring together factory expertise and global distribution into one trusted name.

Guangzhou Augustus Watch Co., Ltd. is a professional manufacturer and processor of quartz watches, mechanical watches, and related timepiece products. We operate with a complete and scientific quality management system that governs every stage of production.

Our credibility, operational strength, and product quality have earned recognition across the industry. We warmly welcome friends and partners from all sectors to visit, inspect our facilities, and explore business opportunities with us.

Operating as a direct factory, we maintain full control over manufacturing quality — from movement selection to final finishing — ensuring every timepiece meets our exacting standards before it reaches your hands.
